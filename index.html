Contact Us

Everyone is welcome here at the Leiden Swimming Club, regardless of age, gender, orientation or skill level. We don't provide swimming lessons but we do provide a safe place to practice technique, share support and meet like-minded people. If you would like to attend one of our meetups, please introduce yourself send an email to info@leidenswimmingclub.com and be sure to include your level of experience. This is particularly important if you are a beginner swimmer as we have to ensure that we have enough experienced members to provide mentorship. Safety is paramount here at Leiden Swimming Club

Our weekly Wednesday evening meetups are located at Zwembad De Vliet, address: Voorschoterweg 6, 2324 NE Leiden
